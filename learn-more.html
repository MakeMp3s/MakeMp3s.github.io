Sync Library keeps your music folders up to date automatically. When new songs get added to a playlist, it only downloads what's missing.

Setting it up:

  • In the main window, click the Sync button To the right of the Download button
  • The Sync Library opens
  • Click Add The Add Playlist to Sync box opens
  • Paste your playlist URL and click Select Folder to choose where the music will be saved
    Select a folder on your computer, or a folder on a plugged-in device like an SSD, phone, or MP3 player.
    Use your playlist's share link button to copy and paste.
  • Click Add Your playlist will appear in the Sync Library list

Keeping it updated: When new music gets added to a playlist, just open the Sync Library and click Sync. It'll detect what's new and add only the missing songs.

Downloading — Standard Method

  1. Copy the link to the playlist you want to download Use your playlist's share link button
  2. Open Make MP3s and click Download
You can queue multiple playlists before starting. Just paste each link before hitting download.

Settings & Features

Cover Art

You can enable or disable Cover Art to display with your music.

Square Crop Cover Art Premium

Crops any rectangular cover art into a perfect square, so your whole collection looks consistent.

Already downloaded without it? No problem. Click Change under Download Location, select the folder you want to fix, then click Square Crop Folder.

Music Video Alternatives

Sometimes a playlist contains music videos, which can mean skits interrupting your listening. Enable this to automatically find audio-only alternatives.

Audio Quality

Choose from four quality settings:

192 kbpsStandard

320 kbpsHQ Max — the standard for high-quality MP3s

Download Location

Choose where your music saves by clicking Change under Download Location.

Auto Volume Premium

Normalizes the volume across all songs so there are no jarring jumps between tracks.

Want to level a folder you've already downloaded? Go to Volume Adjuster, click Select Files, make your selection, and Make MP3s will handle the rest.

Stats After Downloads

Get a summary after each download including success rates, errors, and more.

Updates

Make MP3s checks for updates automatically. You can manually check anytime by clicking Check for Updates.

Account Settings

You can view and manage your account on this website. Click the profile icon in the top right, sign in, then navigate to Account Settings.
