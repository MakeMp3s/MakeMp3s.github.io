Refund Policy

We want you to have a smooth experience with Make MP3s. Because of the nature of digital software, refunds are limited and handled carefully.

Last updated: April 2026

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only considered when both of the following conditions are met:

  • The request is made within 48 hours of purchase
  • The software is not working as intended due to a verified technical issue

Requests made after the 48-hour window will not be considered, regardless of the reason.

What Qualifies for a Refund

  • The software fails to download or convert music properly
  • Core features are not functioning as described on the product page
  • You are unable to use the software despite following the provided instructions

What Does Not Qualify

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Accidental purchases
  • Lack of understanding of features (please review the product page before purchasing)
  • Requests made after the 48-hour window
  • Cases where the software was successfully used

Usage & Abuse Protection

  • Refunds may be denied if excessive or abnormal usage is detected prior to the request
  • We reserve the right to verify usage before issuing any refund

This policy exists to protect the integrity of Make MP3s and ensure fair access for all users. We appreciate your understanding.

Proof of Issue

To process your refund request, we may ask you to provide one or more of the following:

  • Screenshots showing the issue
  • A short screen recording demonstrating the problem
  • A clear written description of what is going wrong

How to Request a Refund

To submit a refund request, please contact us through our feedback page and include:

  • The email address used at checkout
  • A clear description of the issue you experienced

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 24–48 hours.

Agreement

By purchasing Make MP3s, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this refund policy. We encourage all users to review the product features before purchasing to ensure it meets their needs.

Need Help or Have a Question?

If you're experiencing a problem or want to submit a refund request, reach out to our support team. We're here to help and typically respond within 24–48 hours.

Contact Support

Make MP3s is an independent project. We genuinely care about every user's experience and handle all refund requests with fairness and transparency.
